  • Marriages & Children

    1954-1968 – married Mel Ferrer

    1960 – 1st son – Sean

    1969-1982 – married to Andrea Dotti

    1970 – 2nd son – Luca

  • Awards

    1954 – Oscars, Golden Globe Awards & Bafta for Roman Holidays

    1959 – New York Film Critics Awards for The Nun's Story

    1965 – Bafta for Charade

  • Humanitarian Activities

    1989 – Goodwill Ambassador of UNICEF

    Visited Ethiopia, Turkey, Vietnam, Bangladesh, Honduras, Somalia & many other countries with supporting campaigns

    Statue in her honour – The Spirit of Audrey
